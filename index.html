Standard


Time:

Second: According to the International System of Units, a 'Second' is the time it takes for a caesium atom to vibrate 9,192,631,770 times.

Minute: One 'minute' is 60 'Seconds', or how long it takes for a Caesium atom to vibrate 551,557,906,200 times

Moment: One 'moment' is 90 'Seconds', or how long it takes for a Caesium atom to vibrate 827,336,859,300 times

Hour: One 'hour' is 60 'Minutes', or how long it takes for a Caesium atom to vibrate 33,093,474,372,000 times

Day: One 'day' is 12 hours, commonly confused with a 'Calendar Day'

Calendar Day: One 'calendar day' is 24.0000006 (~24) hours, from 12:00 AM to 12:00 AM

Week: One 'week' is 7 'calendar days'

Month: One 'month' is between 28 and 31 'weeks'

Year: One 'year' is 12 'months'/365.242181 (~365) 'days'

Decade: One 'decade' is 10 'years'

Century: One 'century' is 100 'years' or 10 'decades'

Millennia: One 'millennia' is 1,000 'years'/ 100 'decades' / 10 'centuries'

Weight:

Dalton (Atomic Mass): One 'dalton' is roughly the weight of 1 hydrogen atom

Imperial (Avoirdupois):

Grain: One 'grain' is 2.5626e-23 'daltons'

Dram: One 'dram' is 27.3438 'grains'

Ounce: One 'ounce' is 16 'drams' or 437.5 'grains'

Pound (lbs): One 'pound' is 16 'ounces' / 256 'drams' / 7,000 'grains'

Quarter: One 'quarter' is 25 'pound'

Short Hundredweight (cwt): One 'short hundredweight' is 4 'quarters' or 100 'pounds'

Ton: One 'ton' is 20 'short hundredweight' or 2,000 'pounds'

Metric:

Picogram: One 'picogram' is 6.022e+11 'daltons'

Nanogram: One 'nanogram' is 6.022e+14 'daltons' or 1,000 'picograms'

Microgram: One 'microgram' is 6.022e+17 'daltons' or 1,000 'nanograms'

Milligram: One 'milligram' is 6.022e+20 'daltons' or 1,000 'micrograms'

Centigram: One 'centigram' is 6.022e+21 'daltons' or 1,000 'milligrams'

Decigram: One 'decigram' is 6.022e+22 'daltons' or 1,000 'centigrams'

Gram: One 'gram' is 6.022e+23 'daltons' or 1,000 'decigrams'

Decagram: One 'decagram' is 1,000 'grams'

Hectogram: One 'hectogram' is 10 'decagrams' and 100 'grams'

Kilogram: One 'kilogram' is 10 'hectograms' and 1,000 'grams'

Megagram: One 'megagram' is 1000 'kilograms' and 1,000,000 'grams'

Gigigram: One 'gigigram' is 1000 'megagrams' and 1e+9 (10,000,000) 'grams'

Teragram: One 'teragram' is 1000 'gigigrams' and 1e+12 (100,000,000) 'grams'

Volume:

Imperial (Avoirdupois):

Minim: One 'minim' is roughly 0.95 'grains' of 1 drop of water

Fluid Dram: one 'fluid dram' is 60 'minim'

Teaspoon: One 'teaspoon' is 80 'minim'

Tablespoon: One 'tablespoon' is 3 'teaspoons' or 240 'minim'

Fluid Ounce: One 'fluid ounce' is 2 'tablespoons' or 6 'teaspoons'

Shot: One 'shot' is 1.5 'fluid ounces' or 3 'tablespoons'

Gill: One 'gill' is 22⁄3 'shots' or 4 'fluid ounces'

Cup: One 'cup' is 2 'gills' or 8 'fluid ounces'

Pint: One 'pint' is 2 'cups'

Quart: One 'quart' is 2 'pints'

Pottle: One 'pottle' is 2 'quarts'

Gallon: One 'gallon' is 4 'quarts'

Barrel: One 'barrel' is 31.5 'gallons'

Oil Barrel: One 'oil barrel' is 42 'gallons'

Hogshead: One 'hogshead' is 1.5 'oil barrel' or 63 'gallons'

Kelvin: One 'kelvin' is -457.87 'farenheight' or -272.15 'centigrade'

Farenheight: One 'farenheight' is 1/212 of the boiling point of pure water (H2O)

Centigrade: One 'centigrade' is 1/100 of the boiling poit of pure water (H20)

Celcius: One 'celcius' is exactly 1 'centigrade'

Electricity:

Voltage (Volt): One 'volt' is the electric potential between two points of a conductor

Ampere (Amp/Amperage): One 'ampere' is ' the fixed numerical value of the elementary charge e to be 1.602 176 634 × 10−19 ' according to Wikipedia

Wattage (Watt): One 'watt' is voltage times amperage

Force:

Kinetic Energy: Kinetic Energy has no official unit, however is the energy being outputted on any physical object

Potential Energy: Potential Energy has no official unit, however is the possible (potential) energy that can be outputted at one moment in time by a certain object, and alone, cannot do anything physical
